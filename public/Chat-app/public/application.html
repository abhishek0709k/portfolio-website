Application Information

  • A modern, secure, and user-friendly messaging platform for real-time communication.
  • Designed for individuals, groups, and businesses to stay connected seamlessly.

  • Messaging:
    • Real-time text messaging.
    • Message read receipts (delivered and read indicators).
    • Typing indicators to show when someone is replying.
  • Media Sharing:
    • Send images, videos, documents, voice notes, and GIFs.
    • High-quality file sharing with support for large file uploads.
  • Group Chat:
    • Create and manage group chats with customizable group names and icons.
    • Assign admin roles for better group management.
    • Support for unlimited participants.
  • Voice & Video Calls:
    • High-quality one-on-one and group calls.
    • Low bandwidth consumption for smooth connectivity.
  • Customization:
    • Customizable chat themes, backgrounds, and fonts.
    • Dark mode for low-light usability.
  • Search & Filters:
    • Easily search for messages, contacts, and shared files.
    • Filters for unread messages and media.
  • Privacy & Security:
    • End-to-end encryption for secure conversations.
    • Privacy controls: Block/Unblock users, hide last seen, and status updates.
    • Disappearing messages with self-destruct timers.
  • Notifications:
    • Instant push notifications for messages and group activities.
    • Do Not Disturb (DND) mode for better focus.
  • Status & Stories:
    • Post stories and status updates that disappear after 24 hours.

  • AI-Powered Tools:
    • Smart replies to save typing effort.
    • Message translation for global conversations.
    • AI-based chatbots for FAQs and business automation.
  • Offline Messaging:
    • Messages are queued and delivered when the recipient is back online.
  • Data Sync & Backup:
    • Automatic cloud sync across multiple devices.
    • Backup and restore chat history securely.
  • Fun & Interactive Add-Ons:
